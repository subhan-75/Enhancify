About Enhancify

Enhancify is a revolutionary AI-powered photo enhancement platform that transforms ordinary photos into extraordinary images with just one click. Our mission is to make professional-quality photo enhancement accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skills or expensive equipment.

Using next-generation deep learning algorithms, Enhancify automatically analyzes and improves every aspect of your photos. From fixing blur and improving sharpness to enhancing colors and adjusting lighting, our AI delivers stunning results that were previously only possible with professional photo editing software.

Best of all, Enhancify is completely free to use. We believe that everyone deserves to have beautiful, high-quality photos without the hassle of complex editing tools or expensive subscriptions.

Privacy Policy

At Enhancify, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your photos and personal information when you use our service.

Photo Processing

When you upload a photo to Enhancify, it is processed securely on our servers to apply the AI enhancement. Your photos are not stored permanently on our systems after processing is complete. Once your enhanced photo is delivered to you, the original and enhanced versions are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours.

Data Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (such as when contacting us). We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our service, but this data cannot be traced back to individual users.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your photos or personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the enhancement service (such as cloud processing infrastructure). These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at imagecompresserofficial@gmail.com.
